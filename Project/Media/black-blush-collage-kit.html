Description

Decorate your dorm or room with this muted blush collage kit. This collage kit has a chic Paris vibe, with muted black, cream and blush pink tones . Feel free to use however you like, you can overlap the prints, frame them, add in your own photos or anything else you can think of!

Contents: 50 very high quality curated prints on premium 225gsm matte paper. Each print is A4 so 210 × 297 millimetres or 8.27 × 11.69 inches. All prints are portrait orientation.

Attach to the wall with tape, blu-tac, pins, clips or anything else that suits your space. Adhesives are not included due to different requirements for different people. In the pictures I've used masking tape which worked well for me and didn't pull paint off when I took them down. We recommend that you stick all four corners of each print to the wall.


The collage kit will cover an area of 3.12 square meters or 33.6 square foot.

The images in the collage kit have been carefully curated and edited by me. They are a selection of photos taken by me, drawings I've made, vintage prints and open source images. I have the full rights and copyright for the images use. There are a few images that are the same in the different collage kits but have been edited differently.

Please note that the images may appear slightly different on different screens and in real life.
